What's Inside The Magic

Recycled Materials

The lamp incorporates recycled materials such as plastic bottles and cardboard to promote sustainability and reduce waste.

Photo Resistor / Light Sensor

A light-sensitive semiconductor device whose resistance decreases when exposed to light.

Arduino Uno One photoresistor

A photoresistor, also known as an LDR (Light Dependent Resistor), can be easily interfaced with an Arduino Uno to measure light intensity.

10K Ohm resistor

Provides the exact resistance necessary for circuits to work smoothly.

High-Brightness LED Strip

To emit light like a lamp. 12V LED strip.

Breadboard

A solderless electronic prototyping tool used to build and test circuits without soldering.

External Power Supply (12V Adapter or Battery Pack)

Powers the LED strip or multiple LEDs.

Jumper wire

A breadboard is a solderless device used for prototyping electronic circuits.

NPN Transistor

Allows Arduino to control high-power LEDs safely.
